# Company Remote Work Policy (Draft)

## 1. Eligibility
All full-time employees with over 90 days of service are eligible for hybrid work.

## 2. Schedule Requirements
Employees must be in the office Tuesdays and Thursdays.
Remote days are flexible with team lead approval.

## 3. Technology & Security
- Company laptops must be used.
- VPN must be active at all times.
- Public Wi-Fi usage is strictly prohibited without a VPN.

## 4. Contacts
For issues, contact hr@example.com
